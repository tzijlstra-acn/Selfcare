The twist: what I brushed off as a niggle around km 22 was a stress fracture — too much mileage had quietly cracked the fibula in two places, no single misstep involved. Sub-3 was gone, so I jogged the final ~20 km on it and finished anyway, only finding out five days later at the doctor. Not advisable. Not clever. But I finished.

Good to know

The small, true things that make me me.

🧭 Direct, in a Dutch way

It's about efficiency and the right answer — never personal. Push back, bring data, change my mind.

📈 Self-improvement is the whole game

My number-one love. Always trying to get a little better at something.

🧠 Friends outside consulting

Partly shared hobbies, partly to keep hearing genuinely different viewpoints — doctors, architects, people who think in completely different ways.

🏨 Business-trip golden rule

Sleep early, run first thing, then quietly demolish the all-inclusive buffet that's already been paid for. You're welcome.

😴 No drinking at work events

Training reasons — and I love my sleep far too much to trade it.

🚵 Real MVP: Mathieu van der Poel

Keep your Verstappen. The Dutchman who matters rides a bike — MVDP, all day.

🎄 24.12 km every 24 December

A Christmas-morning ritual that earns a genuinely heroic dinner the night before.

☕ Two coffees a day, max

And still, somehow, no espresso machine. A classic thirty-something contradiction.

🧀 Gouda is the best cheese

Not really up for debate, but I'll hear you out.

🛖 A mountain hut I keep up myself

🗣️ Three native languages

Dutch, German, English. My French got rusty despite Paris and Société Générale.

🏔️ Two homes for the heart
